     Elyssa is a graphic/fashion designer based in Naarm/Melbourne. She is an advocate for curiosity, community, and change, specialising in creative direction, typography, and (personal) identity.
    Her interests lay on a spectrum spanning from traditional techniques to new media. She is on the constant search from the ends of the forest to the depths of the world wide web.

Gallery designed and coded by Elyssa, in collaboration with Maggie Xian's Perpetual Sunrise. Resume available upon request.
